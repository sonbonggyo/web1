MOMENT OF INSPIRATION

In 1978 Murakami was in the bleachers of Jingu Stadium watching a baseball game
between the Yakult Swallows and the Hiroshima Carp when Dave Hilton, an American, came to bat. According to an oft-repeated story, in the instant that he hit a double, Murakami suddenly realized that he could write a novel. He went home and began writing that night.
